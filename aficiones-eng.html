Videogames

I've always been an Xbox fan, although it's not very popular in my country. The most important games during my life were on this platform such as Halo, Gears of War, Metal Gear Solid, Resident Evil and FIFA.

Halo
The first game in the series was Halo: Combat Evolved launched for the original Xbox on November 15th of 2001. It was a revolution for the First Person Shooters on a console. Xbos wasn't my first console but I tried this game once I knew that I needed that game. The next revolution was in 2004 when Halo 2 came out, you could play online with your friends and compete in a skill based matchmaking. The most selling game in the franchise was Halo 3 fot the Xbox 360. The last game developed by Bungie was the prequel named Halo: Reach and the following games where developed by 343 Industries.

FIFA
Love and hate. That's my relationship with the FIFA Series. FIFA 09 dethroned Pro Evolution Soccer as a football simulator game. But each year the game is getting worse and worse. FIFA 19 is the worst installment in the series gameplay wise. The game needs and entire rebuilt from the ground in spite of the great sales every year.

Football

The football club of my life is RCD Espanyol from Barcelona, founded in 1900 by Catalan university students. I was in the first stadium when I was a kid, then we went to he Olympic Stadium and finally we got back home in our new stadium at Cornellà-El Prat in 2009. I'm lucky that I have seen them to win two trophies during my lifetime, two national cups in 2000 and 2006. The most important moment in our history was when we arrived to UEFA Cup final in 2007 and we lost in penalty kicks against Sevilla FC. Europe owes us one, maybe it will be the next season. Don't forget what our former player Luís García said: The best thing in Barcelona is to be an Espanyol supporter.

¡Adelante Forza Mágico Espanyol!
